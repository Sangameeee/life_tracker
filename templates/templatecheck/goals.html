Goal Setting Tips

  • Be Specific: Instead of "get fit", write "exercise 30 minutes daily"
  • Make it Measurable: Set clear metrics to track progress
  • Set Deadlines: Give yourself realistic timeframes
  • Break it Down: Large goals should be broken into smaller tasks
  • Review Regularly: Come back to update and refine your goals
